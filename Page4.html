       "Maharashtra Scheme Portal" website has been launched with an objective to provide information of all schemes of government to citizens on a single platform. Here citizens can get the information of schemes implemented by various departments. Scheme related information like related Government Resolution, eligibility criteria, application process, requisite documents, time duration for sanction, concerned officers etc. are available on this website. In addition, information about officials and contact details of field offices of each department have also been provided. This will help citizens to get information about schemes as well as to contact the concerned office for participating in scheme. It will save time and efforts of citizens.
    The main objective of this Government of Maharashtra Schemes Portal application is developing an online application for general public specially for villagers with which they don’t know all schemes that Government provided for them as well as they don’t know how to apply on that schemes. In this Government Schemes Portal application, we are provide you all scheme provided by Government and how you can apply for that scheme easily.
